Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I book a car?

Booking a car is easy! Simply browse our fleet, select your desired vehicle, choose your dates, and complete the booking process online. You can also call our support team for assistance.

What documents do I need to rent a car?

You'll need a valid driver's license, credit card, and proof of insurance. International renters may need additional documentation. Our team will guide you through the requirements.

Can I cancel or modify my booking?

Yes! You can modify or cancel your booking up to 24 hours before your rental start time. Changes made within 24 hours may incur additional fees.

What happens if I have an accident?

All our vehicles come with comprehensive insurance coverage. In case of an accident, contact our 24/7 support team immediately, and we'll guide you through the process.

Do you offer electric vehicles?

Yes! We have a growing fleet of electric vehicles including Tesla, Porsche Taycan, and other premium electric cars. We also provide charging information and support.

What are your operating hours?

Our locations are open from 6:00 AM to 10:00 PM daily. For after-hours pickups or drop-offs, please contact us in advance to make arrangements.
